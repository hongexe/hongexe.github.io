Privacy Policy

Welcome to use our app!

We respect your privacy and never collect or store any of your personal information. Below are instructions on how to use the "Location Permission" "Album Permission":

Location Permission
For apps involving "Location Permission", when using the app for the first time, the user will be asked to agree to open the "Location Permission" or not. The use intention will be explained on the interface. Only when the user selects “agree” that the app will use the user’s location information. All in all, the user's location information is only used locally by the user. We promise never to collect or store it.

Album Permission
For apps involving "Album Permission", when using the app for the first time, the user will be asked to agree to open the "Album Permission" or not. The use intention will be explained on the interface. Only when the user selects “agree” that the app will visit the user’s album. All in all, the user's album is only used locally by the user. We promise never to collect or store it.

This Privacy Policy will be updated and supplemented, but will not change the existing regulations.
This Privacy Policy applies to all applications we develop.

隐私政策

欢迎使用我们的应用！

我们非常尊重您的隐私，绝不收集和存储您的任何个人信息。下面针对应用涉及的“定位”“相册”权限以及如何使用这些权限进行说明。

定位权限
对于涉及定位权限的应用，在首次使用应用的时候，均会征求用户同意是否开启定位权限并说明使用意图，仅当用户选择同意时，应用才会使用您的位置信息，符合苹果的开发规范及隐私设定。对于用户的位置信息仅在用户本地使用，绝不收集与存储。

相册权限
对于涉及相册权限的应用，在首次使用应用的时候，均会征求用户同意是否开启相册权限并说明使用意图，仅当用户选择同意时，应用才会访问您的相册并获取照片，符合苹果的开发规范及隐私设定。对于用户相册中的照片仅在用户本地使用，绝不收集与存储。

本隐私政策会更新补充，但不会更改已有
条例。 本隐私政策适用于所有我们开发的应用。
